Why COSMO-Check?

Viral skincare hacks spread faster than good science. Dermatologists are seeing more burns, pigment problems, and barrier damage from DIY trends — and there’s no simple way for people to check if a trend is safe for their skin. COSMO-Check is here to change that.

Problem

Viral doesn’t mean safe

People copy beauty trends from TikTok and Instagram without knowing the risk. Aesthetic filters hide real skin damage until it’s too late.

Problem

Derm visits after the damage

Dermatologists often only see patients after burns, peeling, and dark marks have already appeared. Most of this harm was preventable.

Solution

Safety checks before you try it

COSMO-Check gives you a clear risk score, expert insights, and safer alternatives before you put anything on your skin.

How COSMO-Check works

1. Search a trend or routine

Type in a viral hack (“microneedling with vitamin C”, “slugging, “skin cycling”) or paste a product combo you’re thinking of using.

2. We analyze risk

Our engine combines ingredient data, dermatology literature, real-world harm reports, and expert reviews to compute a COSMO-Risk Score tailored to your skin profile.

3. You get a clear answer

See how risky the trend is, what could go wrong, what dermatologists say, and which safer alternatives you can try instead.

FAQ

A few quick answers while we build.

Is COSMO-Check a replacement for my dermatologist?
No. COSMO-Check is a safety and education tool, not a diagnosis or treatment service. It’s designed to help you avoid preventable harm and have better conversations with your dermatologist.
How does the COSMO-Risk Score work?
The score is a 0–100 scale combining ingredient safety data, evidence from dermatology, real-world harm reports, and (in the future) dermatologist reviews. It’s meant to be easy to interpret: 0–25 safe, 26–50 use caution, 51–75 risky, 76–100 high damage potential.
Will COSMO-Check tell me what products to buy?
The focus is on safety, not selling. We’ll suggest safer categories or routines (“gentle exfoliant,” “SPF 30+ daily”), and in the future may curate evidence-based options with transparent criteria.
Is COSMO-Check built for all skin tones?
Yes. Preventing hyperpigmentation and barrier damage in deeper skin tones is a core design principle. We aim to highlight how risk differs by skin type and tone, and to bring in dermatologists with expertise in skin of color.
